# DESIGN SPECIFICATION: SH-1500 | Viral Growth & Content Forge
**Status:** FINAL DESIGN | **Phase:** 3 (Solutioning) | **Project:** Sovereign Hub v4.0
**Designer:** Maya [UX/Content]

## 1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY: THE GROWTH HUD
The "Growth" HUD is designed to embody the **Lux Aesthetic 3.0**—a fusion of deep space void, imperial gold accents, and cybernetic cyan glows. It represents the Hive Mind's bridge to the external world, turning raw data into viral momentum.

## 2. KEY COMPONENTS

### A. Viral Momentum Score (VMS) Visualizer
- **Visual:** A large, multi-ring radial gauge with "high-inertia" animation. The needle moves with a weighted bounce, suggesting significant physical mass behind the digital signals.
- **Palette:** 
  - Gauge Track: `rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.05)`
  - Active Fill: Linear gradient (`#D4AF37` to `#00FFFF`)
  - Glow: `drop-shadow(0 0 20px rgba(0, 255, 255, 0.3))`
- **Logic:** Displays the `performance_score` calculated by the VMS Engine.

### B. Social Reach Matrix (3D Grid)
- **Visual:** A perspective-shifted 3D grid (`transform: rotateX(45deg) rotateZ(-20deg)`). 
- **Data Points:** Glowing bubbles of varying sizes.
  - **X-Axis:** Impressions
  - **Y-Axis:** Conversions
  - **Bubble Size:** Viral Momentum (VMS)
  - **Bubble Color:** Platform-coded (Cyan for X, Gold for LinkedIn, White for GitHub).

### C. Marketing Backlog (Content Kanban)
- **Visual:** A 4-column glassmorphism layout (Draft, Optimizing, Ready, Posted).
- **Cards:** 
  - Glass background (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.02)`) with a subtle `blur(16px)`.
  - Content Preview: A truncated "Hook" text.
  - Visual Preview: A small thumbnail of the AI-generated asset.
  - Agent Badges: Tiny glowing pips indicating which agent (Maya/Jordan) last touched the card.

### D. Signal Map (Word Cloud)
- **Visual:** A cluster of keywords floating in the background or a dedicated panel.
- **Interaction:** Keywords pulse with a gold ripple when the "Forge Scout" detects a high-velocity match on social media.

## 3. LUX AESTHETIC 3.0 STANDARDS
- **Void:** `#0A0A0A` (Background)
- **Gold:** `#D4AF37` (Status, Success, Luxury accents)
- **Cyan:** `#00FFFF` (Data, Logic, Connectivity)
- **Typography:**
  - Headlines: `Cinzel` (Serif, Elegant)
  - Body: `Inter` (Sans-serif, Clean)
  - Data/Status: `JetBrains Mono` (Monospaced, Technical)

## 4. TECHNICAL HANDOFF [PHASE 4]
- **VMS Wiring:** Hook `window.growthController.updateVMS(score)` to the SVG dash-offset.
- **Grid Real-time:** Use Supabase real-time listeners on the `marketing_content` table to update bubble positions.
- **Kanban Drag-n-Drop:** Use existing `allowDrop`, `drag`, and `drop` functions modified for the `marketing_content` table.
