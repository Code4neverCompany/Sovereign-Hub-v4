# 🏛️ 4Never Visual Guidelines 3.0 (Official)

## 🎨 Color Palette (The Aureum-Void Standard)

### 1. The Void Base
*   **Void Black (#0A0A0A):** Primary background color. 
    *   *Usage:* Global backgrounds, card surfaces. Not absolute #000000 to allow for texture visibility.
*   **Rich Void (#05050C):** Cold-tinted black for "Receding Background" effects.
*   **Shadow Accent (#121212):** For subtle depth layering and inactive states.

### 2. The Imperial Spectrum
*   **Imperial Gold (#D4AF37 / #C5A059):** Primary brand signal. Metallic, reflective.
*   **Solstice Amber (#F3B340):** A high-vibrancy gold variant for increased contrast.
*   **Rose Copper (#B87333):** A warm transition tone used for physical metallic heat effects in gold-to-void gradients.
*   **Highlight Gold (#FEEA9A):** For extreme light reflections (Peak Highlights).
*   **Champagne White (#E6BE8A):** Warm-tinted white for "Data Pips" and organic energy particles.
*   **Bronze Deep (#856404):** Shadowing within gold textures, bevel effects.
*   **White-Hot Core (#FFFFFF):** Used for the source of energy in kinetic branding.
*   **Incandescent Gold (#FFD700):** Primary silhouette color for energy-based branding.
*   **Deep Amber (#8B4513):** Transitional bloom color for heat/energy effects.
*   **Deep Umber (#2B1A05):** A warm, dark buffer tone used for atmospheric washes.

### 3. Tactical Accents
*   **Holographic Cyan (#00FFFF):** Technical data, terminal outputs.
*   **Event Kinetic Cyan (#00E5FF):** Sharp, high-contrast neon cyan for badges.
*   **Cyber Cyan (#00F2FF):** High-saturation accent for technical precision.
*   **Electric Cyan (#00F2FF):** Primary spark color for innovation focal points.
*   **Powder Blue (#B0E0E6):** A soft, atmospheric transition shade for aesthetic depth.
*   **Electric Amber (#FFBF00):** A high-heat rim light and highlight color for "Sunset-Tech" effects.
*   **Royal Purple (#7000FF):** AI Core activity, navigation active states.
*   **Cyber Purple (#BC13FE):** Vivid neon purple for high-frequency data.
*   **Deep Ochre (#B8860B):** A viscous, grounded base tone for neural circuitry.
*   **Electro-Amber (#FFBF00):** A radiant transmission color for data flow.
*   **White-Hot Incandescence (#FFF8E1):** Peak luminance for neural nodes and intersection points.
*   **Dusty Plum (#3D3642):** A desaturated, cool violet used as a spectral bridge/buffer between amber glows and black void.
*   **Status Green (#22C55E):** System-Operational.

### 4. Shadow Infrastructure

## 📐 Geometric Architecture (The 4Infinite Seal)

### 1. Topological Fusion (The "Shared Spine")
The core brand mark is a **fused monogram of the number '4' and the Letter 'N'**. 
*   **The Shared Spine:** The vertical stem of the '4' MUST serve as the initial stroke of the 'N'.
*   **Continuous Flow:** Ideally, the logo represents a single-path execution.
*   **Kinetische Regel:** In der „Energy“-Version müssen Lichtspuren (Light Trails) in exakten 45-Grad und 90-Grad Winkeln (linearer Beschleunigungs-Vektor) von den Endpunkten und Gelenken des Logos abstrahlen.

### 2. The 4Infinite Ligature (Wordmark Integration)
In comprehensive wordmarks (e.g., "Code4never"), the Infinity symbol (∞) can act as a **structural bridge between the '4' and the 'n'**.
*   **Geometric Flow:** The horizontal crossbar of the '4' extends and loops to form the infinity mark, crossing through its path and emerging as the shoulder of the 'n'.

### 3. Logo Variance & Identification
While the **interconnected 4N monogram** is the fixed hallmark of the brand, its material manifestation is flexible.
*   **Standard:** Imperial Gold dominance.
*   **Aesthetic Variance:** The logo may adopt glass-like translucent properties (Glass-Morphism), internal refractions, or iridescent outcomes as long as the geometric "Shared Spine" remains intact.

## 🎞️ Surface Protocols (Cinematic Tech)

### 1. Luminescent Artery (Glow Protocol)
Glow effects for data streams follow a three-tier bloom:
1.  **Core (1-2px):** Solid #FDFEFF (Kinetic White) for the current.
2.  **Inner Halo (5-10px):** 60% opacity diffusion using Imperial Gold.
3.  **Atmospheric Wash (40-60px):** Soft 10% opacity Gaussian blur.

### 2. Luminance-Bleeding Protocol
Golden elements against black backgrounds must never have a 100% hard aliased edge.
*   **Softening:** A 5-10px bleed at 5-8% opacity into the black.
*   **Base Shadow:** A subtle blue-tinted drop shadow (#05050C) to force the background to recede.

### 3. Technical Mode (The Dev-Shift)
For technical/code-centric interfaces:
*   **Gold Reduction:** Decrease total gold surface area by 80%.
*   **High-Frequency Accents:** Use Cyber Cyan (#00F2FF) and Cyber Purple (#BC13FE) as primary information carriers.

### 4. Creative Saturation (The Spark)
Innovation and creative focal points must utilize the **Electric Cyan (#00F2FF)** spark model.
*   **The Catalyst:** The absolute center of the spark is pure #FFFFFF (White).
*   **Color Pressure:** Saturation peaks at a 5-10px radial periphery of the spark, creating a "Vacuum Radiance" effect.

### 5. Micro-Grain & Telemetry
*   **Noise:** Global 3% monochromatic noise.
*   **Detailing:** Backgrounds must be populated with "Latent Data Elements" (Hex fragments, coordinate pips) in Shadow UI colors.
